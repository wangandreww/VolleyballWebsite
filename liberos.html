Libero

The defender

Libero Highlight Video:

Position Overview:

The libero is the most defensive role of the team. They are responsible for keeping the ball from hitting the ground on their side. The libero is usually the best passer on the team and needs to be very skillful with their platform for passing. They are also the ones trying to pass the balls hit from the spikers and needs to stay low on the ground.

Tips on being a great Libero

  • Staying Low

    • Staying low to the ground is key to being a great Libero. By staying low you are able to spend less time bending down to recieve the fast pace balls that are coming at you. It also helps you set a good foundation for you to be able to absorb the impact of the ball.

  • Keeping Arms Out

    • Keeping your arms out will allow you to set up your passing platform a lot faster. You will be able to just close your forearms the moment you see the ball come towards you. It also increases the chances of the ball hitting your arms even if you are not fast enough to react to the ball.

  • Don't be Afraid

    • Volleyballs do not kill you and will maybe hurt sometimes, but the point is to not be afraid of the ball. Playing scared will impede your ability to pass the ball and cause you to jolt when you pass. When you jolt and pass the ball there is a high chance of you messing up the pass.
