V O L L E Y B A L L By: Andrew Wang, Kevin Tu, James Chen How To Play

Step 1:
Bump

Start the play by recieving the opponents serve and bumping the ball to your setter!

Step 2:
Set

On the second touch, deliver the ball to your spikers by using your hands to set!

Step 3:
Hit

Jump as high as you can to hit the ball onto the opponents' side of the court!
