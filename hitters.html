Hitter/Spiker

The point scorer

Spiking Highlights Video:

Position Overview:

The spiker is the most offensive role of the team. They are responsible for putting the ball down and scoring points for their team. Usually, the spiker needs to have a high vertical jump in order to attack the ball effectively and beat the blockers on the other side.

Tips on being a great Spiker

  • Timing is Key

    • Timing is the single most important thing when trying to spike a volleyball. Try to time your jump so that you hit the ball at the highest point of contact.

  • Keeping your Elbow High

    • Keeping your Elbow High will allow you to reach and stay on top of the ball.

  • Learn to Adjust

    • Adjusting is crucial in becoming a good hitter. Not every set is going to be perfect so make sure that you watch the set before you leave to spike so you know where the ball is going to be.
